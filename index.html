About Me

Hi, my name is Sammy, and I'm a Dungeon Master! I've been a DM for years, and I've created countless DnD Adventures for my friends and family. So, I've decided to share them all. I hugely appreciate your support, as this is a passion project for me. I just hope poeple can use these DnD tools to help them have as amazing of a time as I have. My DnD content is definitely centered around humor, and creating lots of fun situations for players. There will be lots of opportunity for easy improv with these tools, as I feel that is when DnD is at its best. Thank you so much for your support, it means a LOT to me!
